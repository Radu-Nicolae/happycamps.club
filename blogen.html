About us

A family more than a team, a blessing more than a happenstance.
We are Happy Camps Squad, an organization that has become a second home for our over 200 members from Brasov, Bucharest, Sibiu, Cluj and Braila.

A family more than a team, a blessing more than a happenstance.
We are Happy Camps Squad, an organization that has become a second home for our over 200 members from Brasov, Bucharest, Sibiu, Cluj and Braila.
We are those who give teenagers from everywhere value for their leisure time through non formal education, volunteering and, of course, lots of entertainment.
We are those who break the monotony of life and give it colours!
